Contract Title

Blockchain Technology

Conditions:

  • Eligibility: Only students enrolled in recognized educational institutions are eligible to use the wallet.
  • KYC Verification: Users must go through a Know Your Customer (KYC) verification process to ensure their identity and eligibility.
  • Transaction Limits: Set daily or monthly transaction limits to prevent misuse or unauthorized transfers.
  • Wallet Security: Implement robust security measures, such as two-factor authentication (2FA), encryption, and secure password policies.
  • Fund Management: Provide options for students to deposit, withdraw, and transfer funds within the wallet securely.
  • Transaction History: Maintain a transparent and detailed transaction history, allowing students to track their expenditure and income.
  • Conversion Rates: Provide real-time currency conversion rates if the wallet supports multiple currencies for seamless transactions.
  • Budgeting Tools: Include budgeting features to help students manage their finances effectively within the wallet.
  • Integration with Education Institutions: Collaborate with schools and universities to support payments for various academic expenses like tuition fees, textbooks, or event tickets.
  • Payment Reminders: Send notifications or reminders to students for upcoming payment deadlines or overdue payments.
  • Customer Support: Offer prompt and reliable customer support channels to address any issues or queries students may have regarding the wallet.
